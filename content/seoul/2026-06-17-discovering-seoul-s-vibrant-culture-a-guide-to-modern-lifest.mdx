Seoul, the capital of South Korea, is a city that seamlessly blends traditional and modern culture. The MZ generation, which refers to Millennials and Gen Z, plays a significant role in shaping the city's lifestyle and trends. In Seoul, you can experience the latest Korean beauty trends, fashion, and technology.

The MZ generation in Seoul is known for their love of K-beauty, with many popular skincare and makeup products available in the market. Some of the most popular Korean beauty trends include glass skin, which refers to a smooth and luminous complexion, and hanbang, which refers to traditional Korean herbal medicine used in skincare products. For beauty enthusiasts, Seoul is a paradise, with many shops and markets offering a wide range of products, such as **Innisfree** and **Laneige**.

When it comes to fashion, Seoul has several districts that are popular among locals and tourists alike. **Myeong-dong** is one of the most famous shopping districts in Seoul, known for its high-end boutiques and trendy street fashion. Another popular district is **Hongdae**, which is famous for its indie music scene, street art, and vintage shops. For a more unique shopping experience, visitors can head to **Gwangjang Market**, which offers a wide range of traditional Korean clothing and accessories.

Seoul's modern lifestyle is also characterized by its vibrant nightlife, with many bars, clubs, and restaurants available throughout the city. The **Gangnam** district is particularly popular among young people, with its upscale bars and clubs. For a more laid-back atmosphere, visitors can head to the **Itaewon** district, which is known for its international restaurants and bars.

In addition to its modern lifestyle and trends, Seoul is also a city with a rich cultural heritage. Visitors can experience the city's traditional culture by visiting historical sites such as **Gyeongbokgung Palace** and **Bukchon Hanok Village**.
